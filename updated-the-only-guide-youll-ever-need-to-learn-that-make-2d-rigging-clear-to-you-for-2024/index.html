01What is the Meaning of Rigging in Animation?

Alright, so what does rigging 2D characters actually do? What meaning, or effect, does it have? These questions can be quickly answered with this: Rigging (whether its or 3D) is the practice of attaching bones to a character model. Bones that, in the future, you can use to create motion.

If youre an artist, then youre probably familiar with the concept of creating an initial sketch that you put the final drawing on top of done with the sole purpose of creating a structure that will allow you to move forward more easily in your art.

Thats exactly what you do when Rigging. Youre building a structure that will allow you to easily create animated characters that you can move at will in the future.
