However, unlike the inconveniences of using an actual chalkboard, chalkboard animation is not so different from whiteboard animation In fact, its just given a different look. One that may be exactly what youre looking for especially if youre looking to get in touch with the older generation (who would no doubt be more familiar with the concept.)
