Genre: Fantasy

Genre: Science Fiction, Crime

The main characters, such as Major Motoko Kusanagi and the members of Public Security Section 9, are also seen preventing crime in this evolved era. 5. Psycho-Pass

Release Time: 2012

As she discovers what is actually right and wrong, the anime builds suspense in this dystopian-themed anime movie.

6. Your Name

Release Time: 2016
Genre: Sci-fi, drama

Director: Makoto Shinkai

The best anime movie released recently is Your Name. It features a strange phenomenon that leads to the swapping of the lives of two individuals.
