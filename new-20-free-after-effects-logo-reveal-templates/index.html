Dont get me wrong, you dont actually have to be perfect before you go for auditions but make sure you know a thing or two about what youre doing. Make sure you understand what needs to be done, make sure you have a good grasp of what you need to do. Apply for auditions at different places and confidently give them your best. Youre bound to get a gig.
