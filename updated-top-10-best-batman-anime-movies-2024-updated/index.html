However, Joker tricks the Black Mask and destroys all the drug lords along with Black Mask. In all of this, Batman confronts Ras al Gul to know more about the Red Hood and who is underneath it.

7. Batman year one

A lot of comic and anime fans started loving the batman character after this particular anime. Based on the first year of Bruce Waynes life as a batman, the serial shows his trouble with the police and public. 8. Batman: Gotham Knight

No, this is not Dark Knight; it is the sixth sequel of the batman anime series. An underrated DC animated movie. This anime itself is consisting of six chapters. The details in the Jokers dressing and outfit are beyond amazing. Although, the show has some flaws, as the penguin meets Joker a little too early. In search of finding some hidden treasure, the penguin wakes up Dracula.
